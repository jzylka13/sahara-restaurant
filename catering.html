What We Cater

Built around your headcount.

We've catered intimate dinners and 100+ guest events. Tell us how many you're feeding, when, and where — we'll put together a spread.

Most events are served family-style on large platters — the way Yemeni meals are meant to be eaten. Trays, individual portions, and pickup-ready boxes are also available.
